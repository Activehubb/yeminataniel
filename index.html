Profile

Yemi Nathaniel is a global icon, a teacher and tutor. He has worked 25 years closely with the President, Living Faith Church Worldwide as his Personal Assistant, Special Adviser, Executive Secretary in the ministry. He proceeded to serve as registrar and Pro-Chancellor, Covenant and Landmark Universities respectively. In the ministry, he served as National Pastor in Central South Africa.

He is blessed with tremendous insight into the word of God with diverse testimonies as proofs. He speaks with clarity and He is a positive example of his own utterance. Yemi Nathaniel is into raising global leaders of local starters and raising global leaders from local platform through Jesus Christ.
